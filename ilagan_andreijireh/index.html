The so called "post-game" (Initial thoughts, Where I see myself 5 years from.)

The past few months has been a rollercoastr of emotions. To get started, I really thought this was going to be a smooth sailing ride. Yet, I assumed to quickly. Every Saturday, 4:30 PM, I called it "Judgement Day", it brings out the real you, kung worthy ka ba talaga para sa industriya na to.

Few of us already predicted, "Hindi to magiging madali tulad ng ibang sub", "Bagsak yata ako dito", that kind of thinking. Sir Steven showed us a glimpse of this so-called industry. Oo, super hirap. Not for the fragile beings.Pero kahit ganun, super galing mag turo, di nagkulang, clear and precise instructions. I'm very thankful and tbh, lucky na naging prof ko siya.

5 years from now: To be honest, I kinda see myself not working with the industry Sir Steven showed me. I'm at 50-50 kung ano ba talaga destinasyon ko sa buhay na to. A part of me is saying na "Para sayo to, pinili mo to eh, panindigan mo na hanggang dulo" and another saying na: "Mahirap to, mag ibang work ka nalang, shift ka na. Hindi to para sa katulad mo." These past months I've been contemplating to drop his subject due to unforseen events, pero kahit papaano, I still have the will to fight thanks to my emotional support system ^^

What and Where do I want to be? Maybe I'll still be exploring my career choices as I go through life. I don't wanna rush things make another mistake again. I want a career where it is both fun and enjoyable at the same time. Pero the IT industry is still one of my choices to pursue if given the opportunity.

Again, I want to take the opportunity to say, thank you Sir Steven for putting the effort to teach us what this subject is and what industry are we about tackle on if ever na mapili namin to strive for it. Madami ako natutunan not only from educational but a lot of life lessons that you shared every end of the discussions. You made me realize a lot, na hindi lahat ng bagay easy easy lag, lahat pinaghihirapan para makamit ang mga bagay na gusto na makamit ng isang estudyante na tulad ko. Maraming salamat po!
